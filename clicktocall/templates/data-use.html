Data Use Policy

When you enter your phone number, the Call HQ app uses that number to connect you to headquarters.


The app uses Twilio software to route the call. Your number is logged by Twilio and visible to Coworker.org. We only use the list of phone numbers to get a count of how many people use our call-in tool. Once this day of action is over, we'll delete your number from our Twilio logs. We'll never sell your number to a third party or use your number to contact you again.


If you want to subscribe to our mailing list, you can do so by emailing info@coworker.org or signing a petition at Coworker.org.
